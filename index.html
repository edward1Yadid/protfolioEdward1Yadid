about me

Hello! I'm Edward Yadid, a 29-year-old professional currently residing in the beautiful country of Israel. I am a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) and have the privilege of working at EY Israel

While my career as a CPA is deeply fulfilling, I'm also on an exciting journey of self-improvement and growth. I've recently embarked on the path of learning full-stack development, driven by my curiosity and passion for technology. This journey has opened up a world of possibilities, and I'm eager to explore the art of building web applications from both the front-end and back-end perspectives

Beyond the world of finance and coding, I'm a firm believer in the importance of work-life balance. I cherish the moments spent exploring Israel's rich cultural heritage, indulging in its vibrant culinary scene, and, of course, spending quality time with my friends and family

Thank you for taking the time to get to know a bit about me. Whether you're interested in finance, technology, or simply connecting with fellow enthusiasts, I'm always open to new conversations and collaborations
